Frequently Asked

FSW processing time — common questions.

What is the current FSW processing time in 2026?
Based on AORTrack community data, the median FSW processing time (AOR to eCOPR) is approximately 267 days. The P25–P75 window spans roughly 192–315 days. This is community-sourced and not an official IRCC figure.
Why does FSW take longer than CEC?
CEC applicants are Canadian residents (inland), while FSW applicants are typically outside Canada (outland). IRCC processing workflows differ — outland applications involve additional verification steps that add to the overall time. Community data shows a consistent 80+ day gap in 2026.
How does AORTrack track FSW processing time?
Applicants voluntarily submit their AOR date, stream, type (inland/outland), and milestone dates. Data is anonymous and aggregated by cohort month. The median, P25, and P75 values come from real FSW profiles in the community dataset.
Does inland vs outland affect FSW times on AORTrack?
Yes — AORTrack tracks inland and outland separately. The headline figures represent the overall FSW pool. Sign up free to compare your specific inland or outland application against your exact cohort.
Can I compare my FSW application against CEC applicants?
Yes. Our processing stats page shows both streams side by side. The CEC stream page has the full CEC breakdown. Log your profile free and see your percentile vs both streams.

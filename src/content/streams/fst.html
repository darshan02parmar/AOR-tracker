Frequently Asked

FST processing time — common questions.

What is the current FST processing time in 2026?
Based on AORTrack community data, the median FSW processing time (AOR to eCOPR) is approximately 267 days. The P25–P75 window spans roughly 192–315 days. This is community-sourced and not an official IRCC figure.
How does FST compare to FSW and CEC?
FST timelines often sit between CEC and FSW on the community median — longer than typical CEC inland files, somewhat closer to FSW outland patterns.
How does AORTrack track FST processing time?
Applicants voluntarily submit their AOR date, stream, type (inland/outland), and milestone dates. Data is anonymous and aggregated by cohort month. The median, P25, and P75 values come from real FST profiles in the community dataset.
Are FST medicals requested later than CEC? on AORTrack?
Community timelines often show medicals in the Day 90–140 range for FST — later than many CEC medians. Individual files vary.
Is this official IRCC processing time?
Yes. Our processing stats page shows both streams side by side. The CEC stream page has the full CEC breakdown. Log your profile free and see your percentile vs both streams.
